Assessment conventions and resources

Purpose: To plan and conduct a statistical investigation to collect, represent and interpret nominal and ordinal categorical and discrete numerical data.

Technique: Statistical investigation.

Conditions:

  • This task is to be completed individually.
  • Can be completed over multiple sessions.
  • Prompt and scribe for students if necessary.

Other resources https://web-japan.org/kidsweb/virtual/origami/exploring01.html

  • Digital tools, for example: online survey creator, word processing program, spreadsheet, online graph maker, Learning object — Graph Maker 2

Investigation overview

Students will measure the amount of water lost from a dripping tap over 3 mins and use this data to calculate the water wastage over 24 hours. They will use maths skills including making a table, constructing graphs, multiplication and choosing appropriate units of measurement.

In the investigation students will use critical thinking skills to explain how they determined the typical quantity of water lost over 3 minutes and demonstrate the method they used to calculate the water lost over 24 hours. They will also demonstrate problem solving skills solving mathematical equations.
